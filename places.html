Places & Maps

The Colony

A 3-floor, 19-room dungeon deep in a thick forest with walls made of entwined living branches and vines.

Religious colony that succumbed to a massacre many years ago, still contains the remains of its defendants and their personal effects. Shrine to Phayrd, a dryad in mourning, a wereboar

Source: Blood of Gruumsh
Abandoned Temple

A tactical map of an abandoned temple.

The temple was originally built for Erathis but after her acolytes were slaughtered, cultists rededicated it to the evil god Orcus. Murals, statue of a huge demon, bloody sacrifice, hidden cache, and several bookcases.

Source: Dead by Dawn
Temple Rectory

A tactical map of a rectory full of undead.

The rectory contains the Necroshard and the reanimated bodies of a foolish necromancer and a party of ruthless ratcatchers.

Source: Dead by Dawn
The Village of Baron's Hill

A village nestled among some hills with an old manor and barrow mounds around.

A village with an inn, an abandoned manor house, a general store and a small cemetery. Villagers are fleeing because the color has disappeared from the area.

Source: Fall of the Gray Veil
Grygori's Cave

A 9-room dungeon carved into a creekside bank.

The home of a reclusive necromancer's minion, the dungeon is filled with traps and insects.

Source: Fall of the Gray Veil
Yornhaven

A 29-room dungeon for a tomb-turned-necromancer's-lab.

Words words words words words words words words words words words words words words words.

Source: Fall of the Gray Veil
A Timely Rescue

A tactical map of a village including refugee tents.

Tactical encounter involves defending refugees in the village square from cultists. Includes tents.

Source: Heathen
Village Showdown

A tactical map of a large house in the middle of a sparse forest.

Tactical encounter involves defending a house against bugbears and hobgoblins.

Source: Heathen
Blades in the Night

The foundation pit of an ancient temple with pillars around it offering cover and a 5-foot drop.

Tactical encounter involves dealing with 4 doppelgangers, one of whom assumes the shape of a familiar NPC.

Source: Heathen
Hunter and Hunted

Ambush on a winding road.

5 Shadow Hounds wait to assault unsuspecting travelers.

Source: Heathen
Fire on the Water

Houses on fire on the edge of the riverbank.

Dragonborn soldiers, a death mage and a bugbear ransacking a couple of hamlets.

Source: Heathen
Friend of My Enemy

A riverboat moored on a sheltered shale beach.

The rocky boat and the beach become the battleground for a Tiefling Darkblade attack.

Source: Heathen
Temple of Naarash

A 7-area dungeon depicting a mountain temple.

The temple of Naarash was built by a lost race of giants. Now it is swarming with constructs, cultists, and Jaryn's personal guard.

Source: Heathen
