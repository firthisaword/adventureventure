The Chaos Scar

The Chaos Scar is a sandbox champaign setting for D&D 4e. It is designed in the spirit of Keep on the Borderlands, the famous AD&D 1e sandbox setting. The Chaos Scar has no overarching plot line. Instead, it consist of a number of short adventures (heroic and early paragon tier) set in the Valley of the Chaos, where an evil meteor landed a long time ago.
