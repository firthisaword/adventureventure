Adventure Venture

Adventure Venture is a proof-of-concept prototype for a repository of existing roleplaying game adventures. It was created in response to user experience research with game masters on Reddit. It is interesting in the following ways:

No searching, no filters
Find and steal adventure hooks, plots, people, and places
Create and share custom collections with DMs or players
See official and user-made adventure paths and campaigns
